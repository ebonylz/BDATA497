Hi, I'm Ebony.

I am a Data Visualization student at the University of Washington Bothell with a strong interest in turning complex data into clear and engaging visual stories. I have experience working with R, Java, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, and I enjoy cleaning data, exploring patterns, and building visualizations that make information easier to understand. My work focuses on combining data analysis with visual storytelling, with an emphasis on clarity and usability. As I continue toward graduating in Spring 2026, I’m especially interested in interactive dashboards, geographic data analysis, and creating visualizations that help people make informed decisions.

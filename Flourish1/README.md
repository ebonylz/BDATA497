This visualization shows the average hourly wages of City of Seattle employees, broken down by gender and race. It compares how much male and female employees earn across different racial groups, making it easier to see patterns or disparities in pay. Overall, the chart highlights that women tend to earn slightly less than men across most racial categories, although the differences vary by group.

The data comes from the City of Seattle Staff Demographics dataset (2026), which includes employee wage and demographic information. This dataset represents average hourly wages and may be influenced by differences in job roles or sample sizes within each group.
